Stylish and compact, 10.1 megapixels camera

The 10.1-megapixel EX-Z1000 allows you to capture every detail of your special moments. Its advanced EXILIM Engine, paired with a larger 1/1.8-inch CCD, delivers exceptional image quality by highlighting the delicate nuances of your subjects. Plus, it features a sleek and compact design, a hallmark of the entire EXILIM series.

Wide, high-brightness 2.8" LCD

The wide, high-brightness 2.8" monitor screen (14:9 aspect ratio) enhances images, making them vibrant and lively. With its high-definition 230,400-pixel liquid crystal display, it showcases intricate details in both images and text. A stripe array LCD makes text clearer for easier reading of document images. The camera can automatically adjust the screen brightness based on the available light, or you can choose from four preset brightness levels.

4.3x to 17.1x zoom without degradation of image quality

The 10-megapixel CCD paired with the EXILIM Engine allows for non-deterioration zoom, delivering image quality comparable to optical zoom. This feature extracts the necessary portion from the largest available image size, ensuring close-ups remain clear and free from the degradation typically seen with digital zoom.

Capture 360 images on just one charge

With the EXILIM Engine and Super Life Battery, you can capture 360 images (CIPA Standard) on a single charge. Alternatively, you can enjoy 13 hours of uninterrupted image playback on the camera.

Anti Shake DSP reduces the effects of hand or subject movement

The EXILIM Engine's Anti Shake DSP (Digital Signal Processor) enhances sensitivity and increases shutter speeds, effectively reducing the impact of hand and subject movement, even in low-light conditions.
