About Us

Our History

Smokin’ Burger started as a small roadside grill in 2012 nestled in a cozy town where flavor came first. What began as a passion project—serving flame-grilled burgers to friends and neighbors—quickly turned into a must-visit spot for burger lovers.

Our Founders

Behind Smokin’ Burger are two lifelong friends, Linguini and Remy, whose love for smoky flavors and quality ingredients led them to create a burger experience like no other.

Our Mission

At Smokin' Burger, our mission is simple: serve unforgettable burgers with passion and purpose We focus on bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and a welcoming atmosphere, making sure that every bite tells a story.
