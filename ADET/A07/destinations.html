Explore Japan in 360°

Japan 360° allows you to experience the breathtaking sights of Japan from the comfort of your own home! With interactive 360° views of famous Japanese locations, you can immerse yourself in the beauty and culture of Japan, whether you’re planning a trip or just curious about the places. This site uses Pannellum, a powerful panoramic viewer, to bring stunning 360° images directly to your screen. All you need to do is select the location you wish to explore, and you're ready to go on a virtual adventure!

How to Use
  • 1. Simply choose a place from the dropdown menu.
  • 2. Once you’ve selected a place, use your mouse or touchpad to drag around and look in any direction.
  • 3. A small compass in the corner helps you orient yourself with the panoramic view.
  • 4. Use the zoom slider to get a closer or wider view of the location.

The more you explore, the more you’ll discover! From iconic landmarks to hidden gems, this site brings you closer to Japan’s most famous spots.
