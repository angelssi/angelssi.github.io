NewJeans (뉴진스) is a South Korean teenage girl group,
which debuted on July 22, 2022 under ADOR. It consists of
five members: Hanni, Minji, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.

Profile

HAERIN

Kang Haerin is a South Korean singer-songwriter and dancer under ADOR and HYBE Labels. She's Known for her cat-like visuals and charms. Hearin is also credited as one of the songwriters for NewJeans' with popular songs "NewJeans" and "Super Shy."

HANNI

Hanni Phạm is a Vietnamese-Australian singer-songwriter, dancer, and composer under ADOR and HYBE Labels. She participated in the 'Plus Global Audtion' and became a trainee under Source Music. Eventually, she joined ADOR as a member of NewJeans.

HYEIN

Lee Hyein is a South Korean singer and dancer under ADOR and HYBE Labels. Hyein began to train under ADOR until making her official debut as the youngest member of NewJeans on July 22, 2022. Hyein started as a child model for different brands and fashion shows.

MINJI

Kim Minji is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, and composer under ADOR and HYBE Labels. Born in Chuncheon, South Korea, Minji moved to Seoul and began training in Source Music. Following this, Minji joined the label ADOR as a member of NewJeans.

DANIELLE

Danielle June Marsh is an Australian-South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, and voice actress under ADOR and HYBE Labels. Danielle briefly lived in Korea where she became a child model and appeared in reality programs with her family. Later on, she joined ADOR as a member of New Jeans.
