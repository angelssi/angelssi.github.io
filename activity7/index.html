Naruto (ナルト)

Explore the exciting world of Naruto, a famous manga and anime created by Masashi Kishimoto. Naruto Uzumaki is a young ninja with a powerful force hidden inside him,va secret that has made him feel isolated from his village. His biggest dream is to become the Hokage, the strongest ninja and village leader, but first, he needs to pass his exams! Despite struggling with basic ninja skills, Naruto’s determination to succeed is his greatest strength. Joined by the talented Sasuke, smart Sakura, and their leader Kakashi, Naruto sets off on his journey to become Hokage. Along the way, he faces outside threats to the Hidden Leaf Village and realizes he must grow much stronger to achieve his dream and protect his friends. Naruto is loved for its deep characters, complex storylines, and thrilling fights. The Naruto universe goes beyond the main series, with spin-offs like "Boruto: Naruto Next Generations," along with many video games, movies, and books.

Created by Masashi Kishimoto

MANGA

Naruto is a Japanese manga series by Masashi Kishimoto, following Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja aspiring to become the Hokage, the strongest ninja in his village. Throughout his journey, he faces challenges, grows stronger, and forms bonds with friends and rivals.

NOVEL

The Naruto novels expand on the world, characters, and lore of the Naruto series, exploring untold stories, side characters, and post-series events. They delve into character backstories, provide insights into the Naruto universe, and bridge the gap between the original series and its sequel, Boruto.

GAME

Naruto Games are a diverse collection of titles based on the popular anime and manga series. They span various genres, focusing on role-playing and action-adventure gameplay. Players can engage in battles using various characters with unique jutsu and abilities.

ANIME

Naruto also has anime series based on Masashi Kishimoto's manga, originally airing from 2002 to 2017. Known for its vibrant animation and well-choreographed battles, Naruto has left a lasting cultural impact, inspiring numerous spin-offs, movies, and games.
